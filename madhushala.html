Madhushala

Author: Written by by Harivansh Rai Bachchan

Description:Madhushala is a classic poetry book written by the renowned and revered poet Shri Harvansh Rai in 1935 (Bachchan 2003). The poetic narrative symbolises life as a tavern or a public drinking place. These are incidentally known as bars and pubs now-a-days, though one still comes across taverns in many parts of the world.
