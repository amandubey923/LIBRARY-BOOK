Madhushala

Author: Harivansh Rai Bachchan

Madhushala is a famous Hindi poetry collection, first published in 1935. It uses symbols like the tavern (madhushala), wine, and cupbearer to express ideas about life, love, spirituality, and the human journey. The book is known for its lyrical style and philosophical depth and remains one of the most celebrated works in Hindi literature.
