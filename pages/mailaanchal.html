Maila Anchal

Author: Phanishwar Nath Renu

Maila Anchal (1954) is a famous Hindi regional novel that portrays the rural life, culture, poverty, social problems, and political changes in villages of Bihar after India's independence. The story realistically depicts the struggles and aspirations of common villagers.
