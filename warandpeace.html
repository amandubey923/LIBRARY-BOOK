War-and-Peace

Author: Novel by Leo Tolstoy

Description: War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy is an 1867 novel set between the years 1805 and 1813. It follows several different Russians and their families and the events leading up to and during the 1812 invasion of Russia by France. The three main families are the Rostovs, the Bolkonskys, and the Bezukhovs.
